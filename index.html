Guiding values
The design of my pattern recognition game was guided by simplicity, clarity, and engagement. I wanted players to experience a smooth, intuitive interface that focused on memory and reaction rather than visual clutter. These values were embodied in the UI through a minimal grid layout, consistent color palette, and gentle sounds for feedback. The soft transitions from each sequence and subtle sound cues reinforced progress and mistakes without overwhelming the user, creating a calm yet stimulating environment. Each design choice, was made to enhance accessibility and maintain a sense of flow, ensuring that users could focus entirely on recognising and remembering the patterns.
